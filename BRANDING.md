## Primary Tagline

### "Diamonds or Silence"

- **Structure:** Mercedes binary ("The Best or Nothing")
- **Diamonds:** Hardest, most precious, luxury pinnacle, clarity, cut precision
- **Silence:** Audio negative space — the only alternative to excellence
- **Energy:** Uncompromising. Precious or mute. No middle ground.
